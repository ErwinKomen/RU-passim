Getting started

The passim application has been designed by the Technical Service Group of the Humanities Lab at the Radboud University Nijmegen. Any researcher is invited to register (via sign up) and maintain his or her own research projects. Projects of one researcher are by default not visible to other researchers, but it is possible to specify a different accessibility level.
