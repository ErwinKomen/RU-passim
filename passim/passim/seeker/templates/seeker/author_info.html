Authorship

The Author can have the following values:

  • Undecided: default mode, a decision needs to be made by the PASSIM editors
  • Dubius: there is no consensus in scholarship, but ‘real’ authors are posited
  • Anonymus: no author or only pseudo-authors have been associated with the sermon in scholarship
  • Name: only one real person is associated with the sermon OR there is a consensus in scholarship
